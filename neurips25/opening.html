Opening: Why Power and Energy? And Why Now?

Speaker

Jae-Won Chung University of Michigan

Abstract

Recently, we've been seeing the word power and energy more and more often, especially together with AI. What's going on? Why are they important? Is this issue here to stay?

We will open the tutorial by answering these questions, divide the issue into three key sessions (proper measurement, power implications on performance, and performance-aware energy optimization), and introduce our tutorial speakers.
